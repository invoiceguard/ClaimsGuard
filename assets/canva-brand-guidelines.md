## Canva Design Specs

### Logo Requirements

**Primary Logo (Horizontal):**
- Dimensions: 1200 × 400 px
- Safe zone: 100px padding all sides
- Background: Transparent PNG + White background version

**Icon Only (Favicon/App):**
- Dimensions: 512 × 512 px
- Simple, recognizable at 16×16px

**Colors:**
| Use | Hex | Name |
|-----|-----|------|
| Primary | #1a365d | Deep Navy |
| Secondary | #38a169 | Growth Green |
| Accent | #e53e3e | Alert Red |
| Light | #f7fafc | Cloud White |
| Dark Text | #2d3748 | Slate |

## Logo Concepts to Try in Canva

### Concept 1: Shield + Claim Document
- Shield shape (trust/security)
- Stylized document/claim form inside
- Checkmark or magnifying glass overlay

### Concept 2: CG Monogram
- Interlocking C and G
- Medical cross or dollar sign integration
- Clean, geometric, modern

### Concept 3: Radar/Scan + Dollar
- Circular scan/radar waves
- Dollar sign or claim document in center
- "Finding what others miss" concept

### Concept 4: Magnifying Glass + Shield
- Classic magnifying glass
- Shield shape as lens
- Subtle medical/claim imagery

## Canva Steps

1. **Create Account:** Use fast@claimsguard.io
2. **Dimensions:** Custom size → 1200 × 400 px (logo)
3. **Elements to search:**
   - "shield"
   - "magnifying glass"
   - "document"
   - "checkmark"
   - "medical cross"
   
4. **Font Pairings:**
   - Headline: Montserrat Bold or Poppins Bold
   - Tagline: Inter Regular or Open Sans Light

5. **Export:**
   - PNG (transparent)
   - PNG (white background)
   - SVG (if available on Canva Pro)
   - JPG (for social/printing)
